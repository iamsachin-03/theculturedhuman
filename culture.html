Delectable Cuisine

Litti Chokha

The heart of Bihari cuisine, these roasted wheat balls filled with spiced sattu are a culinary icon.

Sattu Paratha

A wholesome and protein-rich flatbread, perfect for a power-packed breakfast.

Thekua

A crisp, sweet biscuit, traditionally made as a prasad during the Chhath Puja festival.

Iconic Places to Visit

Mahabodhi Temple

The sacred site in Bodh Gaya where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment. A UNESCO World Heritage site.

Nalanda University

Stroll through the ruins of one of the world's oldest and greatest universities.

Glass Bridge, Rajgir

Experience a thrilling walk on India's first glass skywalk amidst scenic hills.

Pearls of Wisdom (Padhua)

"अधजल गगरी छलकत जाय"

An empty vessel makes much noise; people with little knowledge boast the most.

"जेकर लाठी, ओकर भঁইস"

He who holds the stick, owns the buffalo; might is right.

"घर के मुरगी दाल बराबर"

Self-possessions are always undermined; familiarity breeds contempt.

"नाच ना जाने, आँगन टेढ़"

Doesn't know how to dance, but blames the crooked courtyard; a bad workman blames his tools.

"सौ सोनार के, एक लोहार के"

A single blow from a blacksmith is as effective as a hundred from a goldsmith.

"बन्दर क्या जाने अदरक का स्वाद"

What does a monkey know of the taste of ginger; casting pearls before swine.

"केतनो सोझ करू, कुकुर के पुछ टेढ़े रहत"

You can't straighten a dog's tail; some people will never change.

"आगे नाथ ना पीछे पगहा"

With no one to control them; someone who has no responsibilities.

"चमड़ी जाये पर दमड़ी ना जाये"

The skin may go, but the penny shouldn't; about a very miserly person.

"जबरा मारे रोवे ना दे"

The powerful one hits you and doesn't even let you cry; oppression by the powerful.

Music, Dance & Art

Melodies and Rhythms

Folk songs like Sohar (celebrating birth) and Sumangali (for weddings) are integral to life. The poignant Bidesia dance-drama and the energetic Jat-Jatin of Mithila tell stories of love, separation, and daily life.

Madhubani Painting

Originating from the Mithila region, this world-renowned art form is characterized by its intricate geometric patterns and vibrant natural colors.
